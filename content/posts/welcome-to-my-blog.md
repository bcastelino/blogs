## Hello there

This is the first post on my new personal blog. I built it to have a quiet corner
of the internet where I can write about whatever I'm thinking about — technology,
projects I'm building, things I'm learning, and the occasional tangent.

## What to expect

I write about a mix of topics:

- **Engineering** — the things I build and the lessons learned along the way.
- **Tools & workflow** — small improvements that compound over time.
- **Ideas** — half-formed thoughts that I want to think through in writing.

> Writing is thinking. The act of putting words down forces clarity.

## How this site is built

This blog is a statically exported **Next.js** site, hosted for free on **GitHub Pages**.
Posts are written in plain Markdown, so adding a new one is as simple as dropping a
`.md` file into the `content/posts` folder.

```bash
# add a new post
content/posts/my-new-idea.md
```
